About my Project

I can't tell you everything, but...

I have been writing and drawing a hybrid graphic-novel for the better part of a year. As of today, I have completed what I would say is half of the draft, editing the writing as I go and building the world, one structure, one plotline at a time.

As a space opera in the works, I have created a number of science-fiction technologies based on real world physics and meta-properties as a way to create a uniformed impression of the different societal classes that exist in this Solar System.
