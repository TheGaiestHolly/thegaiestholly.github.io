Assessments

Visual Media Design

Compositional Analysis

Western Sydney University

In this assessment, it was my task to identify four famous photographs and discuss the compositional techniques that defined their quality. This process helped me learn more about photographic practice and how I could apply it within my creative career.

Through my learning, I discovered new ways to position the subject of my art to envoke emotion in the viewer and create a stronger sense of empathetic importance.

Portfolio Draft Action Plan

Western Sydney University

To continue, this assessment required me to produce five original photographs forming a visual narrative. The goal was to create an advertisement aimed at international and domestic students interested in travelling within the NSW region. As such, my proposed narrative focused on the theme of disconnection from technology and reconnection with nature.

Digital Photographic Portfolio

Western Sydney University

Ultimately, the last two assessments led to this: my Digital Photographic Portfolio. To highlight its contents, I was tasked with discussing two photographers, one historical and one contemporary, Diane Arbus and Nathalie Ghanem-Latour, who isnpire me and have influenced my work. Later, I reconfigured my images to maximise their potential as advertising elements, discussing both the client brief and target audience.

Historical Image Maker Presentation

Western Sydney University

The final assessment in my photography class required me to discuss the life and influence of Diane Arbus. I explored her photographic practice, highlighting the deeply human nature of her work and moral character.

Website Design

Compositional Analysis

Western Sydney University

As a group, two of my peers and I presented our interpretation on an Arnold Schwarzeneggar website. Given three scenarios, each of us was tasked with identifying the underlying issues, proposing solutions to address them, and presenting the final outcome of our fixes.

Website Analysis

Western Sydney University

I was tasked with finding a portfolio website created by a professional within my chosen industry. This involved exploring the various websites available to their customer base, providing recommendations for improving specific aspects of their site, and discussing the overall aesthetic. For this assessment, I focused on the work of Paul Demillier.
